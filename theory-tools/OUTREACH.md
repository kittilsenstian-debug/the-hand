## Hard Lessons (from Baez & Andersen)

1. **ONE email. ONE question. Then WAIT.** Don't send 4 messages in 3 days.
2. **Lead with the strongest claim, not the weakest.** η=α_s and 9 sig figs of α, not μ/3.
3. **Never say "derives everything."** Say "three specific coupling constants."
4. **Never say "I can't tell if it's real."** Say "I'm actively trying to break it."
5. **Dimensionless only.** Never compare a dimensionless number to a frequency without the full formula.
6. **Target the right person.** Andersen (QCD renormalization) ≠ modular forms in gauge theory. Check their actual papers.
7. **Ask a question they can answer in 5 minutes.** Not "check out my framework."
