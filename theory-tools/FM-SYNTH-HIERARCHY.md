## FM Synthesis Recipe

In FM synthesis, a carrier signal has its frequency modulated by a
modulator signal:

```
output(t) = A * sin(2*pi*f_carrier*t + I * sin(2*pi*f_modulator*t))
```

where I is the modulation index (depth of modulation).
