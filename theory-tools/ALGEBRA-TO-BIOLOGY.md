## PART I: WHAT DIED

### 1. μ/3 = 612 THz is DEAD as a derivation

**The claim:** The proton-to-electron mass ratio divided by 3 gives the aromatic consciousness frequency.

**Why it's dead:**
- **Unit-dependent.** μ = 1836.15 is dimensionless. μ/3 = 612.05 is a dimensionless number. To get "612 THz" you multiply by 1 THz = 10¹² Hz — an arbitrary SI prefix choice. In GHz it gives 0.612 GHz (meaningless). The "match" only works if you happen to choose THz as your unit. *This is dimensional nonsense.*
- **Benzene doesn't absorb at 613 THz.** The Hückel calculation gives benzene's HOMO-LUMO gap = 4.90 eV = 1184 THz — almost exactly DOUBLE μ/3. Individual aromatics (tryptophan 1054 THz, phenylalanine 1153 THz, tyrosine 1090 THz) absorb at 250-290 nm (UV), NOT at 489 nm.
- **613 THz is a COLLECTIVE mode.** The Craddock 2017 value is a collective London dispersion oscillation among 86 aromatic amino acids in the tubulin dimer, computed by DFT. It is NOT a single-molecule property.

**Status:** REMOVE from derivation claims. Replace with honest formulations below.

### 2. 40 Hz = 4h/3 is DEAD as a derivation

**The claim:** 40 Hz gamma frequency = 4 × (E₈ Coxeter number 30) / 3.

**Why it's dead:**
- **Pure number without units.** 4×30/3 = 40, not 40 Hz. No dimensional argument connects them.
- **Reverse-engineered.** Found by factoring 40 using h=30, not derived from any principle.
- **h=30 generates many brain rhythms equally "naturally":** h/3=10 (alpha), h/4=7.5 (theta), h²/60=15 (beta), 2h=60 (high gamma).
- **40 Hz is set by biology.** GABA_A receptor kinetics (τ ≈ 5-10 ms from PV+ interneurons) and cortical E-I loop architecture. Well-established computational neuroscience (Wang & Buzsáki 1996).
- **No route from PT n=2 eigenvalue.** The breathing mode gives ω₁ = √3 in kink units. To get 40 Hz, need mass scale ~10⁻¹³ eV — below every particle physics scale by 10+ orders of magnitude.

**Status:** REMOVE from derivation claims. Rate as COMPATIBLE (real frequency, no framework derivation).

### 3. μ/18 = 102 THz = O-H stretch is DEAD as a derivation

Same unit-dependence problem as μ/3. The O-H stretch frequency IS related to proton mass through Born-Oppenheimer (f_vib ~ f_el/√μ), but this is trivially true for ALL molecular vibrations. Not framework-specific.

### 4. V(Φ) cannot select water or aromatics

- **Scale gap:** V(Φ) operates at electroweak scale (~125 GeV). Biology at molecular scale (~2.5 eV). Ratio ~ 3×10⁷. No spatial overlap between wall's PT bound states and any molecule.
- **No chemical specificity:** V(Φ) produces coupling constants. Nothing in the algebra predicts that hexagonal carbon rings with delocalized π-electrons should exist or be special.
- **Water's uniqueness follows from {α, μ, chemistry}**, not from V(Φ) specifically.
