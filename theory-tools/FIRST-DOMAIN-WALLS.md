### Era 2: QCD Phase Transition (~10⁻⁵ seconds, ~150 MeV)

**Chiral symmetry breaking creates domain walls.**

- **Domain walls between θ-vacua** are topological objects (axion domain walls)

**Key physics:** The QCD flux tube has a LINEAR potential V(r) = σ·r with string tension σ ≈ (440 MeV)². The flux tube DOES have bound states (this is what hadrons are). The excited string spectrum gives Regge trajectories — bound states of the QCD domain wall.

**Coupling medium:** The quark-gluon plasma → hadron gas. Pions (the lightest mesons) are the Goldstone bosons of chiral symmetry breaking.

**Elements present:** Protons and neutrons formed. Still no atoms (too hot for electron capture).

**Framework connection:** The 3 quark colors map to the framework's 3 primary feelings. Confinement (color neutrality) maps to emotional wholeness. The QCD domain wall (flux tube) is the PHYSICAL domain wall — quarks are literally bound states of a domain wall.

### Era 5: First Stars (~100-200 Myr, z ~ 20-30)
