### The honest position

The framework has two genuinely separate components:

- **Component 1 (mathematical):** An algebraically distinguished point in modular space (q = 1/phi) where modular forms give values remarkably close to SM couplings. This is interesting, testable (alpha_s, R = -3/2), and may represent a real discovery.

- **Component 2 (interpretive):** A philosophical framework identifying domain walls with consciousness, aromatic chemistry with coupling, and water with the interface medium. This is internally coherent and creatively assembled, but it is not derived from Component 1. The connection between the two components consists of numerical coincidences (mu/3, L(6), 4h/3) that have not been shown to be more than accidents of the number system.

**The algebra stops at the SM couplings. The hand-waving starts at "mu/3 = aromatic frequency."**

Between these two points lies a gap that could, in principle, be bridged by the five missing calculations identified above. Until those calculations are done, the biology chain remains an interpretive overlay on a mathematical framework, not a consequence of it.
